London

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom. For two millennia, it was a large town and its history goes back to its foundation by the Romans, when it was named Londínio. The center of London, the former City of London, also known as The Square Mile ("the square mile") or The City, maintains its medieval borders. At least since the 19th century, the name "London" has referred to the metropolis developed around this nucleus. Today, most of this conurbation forms the Greater London region, whose administrative area has its own elected mayor and assembly.

Amsterdan

Amsterdam is the capital and most populous city in the Kingdom of the Netherlands. Its status as the Dutch capital is guaranteed by the Constitution of the Netherlands, although it is not the seat of Dutch government, which is in The Hague. The city is located in the province of North Holland, in the west of the country. It comprises much of the northern part of the Randstad, one of the largest conurbations in Europe, with a population of approximately 7 million.

Berlin

Berlin is the capital and one of the sixteen states of Germany. With a population of 3.5 million within city limits, it is the country's largest city, and the seventh most populated urban area in the European Union. Situated in northeast Germany, it is the center of the Berlin-Brandenburg metropolitan area, which includes 5 million people from over 190 nations. Located on the great European plain, Berlin is influenced by a temperate seasonal climate. About a third of the city's area is made up of forests, parks, gardens, rivers and lakes.

São Paulo

São Paulo is a Brazilian municipality, capital of the homonymous state and the main financial, corporate and commercial center in South America. It is the most populous city in Brazil, the American continent, the Portuguese-speaking world and the entire southern hemisphere. São Paulo is the most influential Brazilian city on the global stage, being, in 2016, the 11th most globalized city on the planet, receiving the alpha global city classification by the Globalization and World Cities Study Group & Network (GaWC). The motto of the city, present in its official coat of arms, is Non ducor, duco, a Latin phrase that means "I am not led, I lead".
